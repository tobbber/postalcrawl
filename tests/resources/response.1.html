UNIC Modular Glass Showcases

SOLID & DURABLE • ELEGANT • MANY VARIED DESIGNS TO CHOOSE FROM

Formedge has a reputation for making high quality products and UNIC is definitely one on the list. We use high quality aluminium rounded profiles of 1.5mm thickness with cast iron connector couplings to make the structures, 4mm, 6mm and 8mm thick clear glass with flat edge finishing for the side infills, tops and shelves. The whole system is inter-lockable and detachable for knock-down packaging and for replacing of broken glass components when there’s need for it.

We make our very own unique adjustable light fittings with concealed wiring that run along the side profiles. The bulb head is movable to achieve maximum light coverage to highlight items on display and yet it’s nicely tucked away in the corners of these showcases.
